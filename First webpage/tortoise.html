Toby The Grumpy Tortoise

A 50 year old grumpy tortoise


He is:

  • A very huge tortoise measuring 1 metre long
  • Slow with a top walking speed of 0.25 miles per hour
  • A strict herbivore with hibiscus being his favourite flower

He dislikes:

  1. The rain
  2. Having a shower
  3. Spinach
